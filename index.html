About Me

Hi there! I am an 2nd year PhD student of Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Ohio State University. My research interests focus on blockchain security, as well as AI-related topics such as model collaboration, post-training, and AI safety. I'm always open to collaborations. If you want to share any thoughts, or our interests naturally overlap, please feel free to get in touch with me!

I interned at CertiK in the summer of 2025, under the mentorship of Dr. Zhaofeng Chen and Prof. Kang Li. During my undergraduate study, I mainly work in Security PRIDE research group under Prof. Haoyu Wang at HUST. Also, I have the fortune to work with Prof. Gareth Tyson at HKUST and Prof. Konstantin Beznosov at UBC.
